Maddison World Economy 2018

The graphs and plots on this page propose a way to visualise the data collected by Maddison World Economy work (https://www.stat.berkeley.edu/~aldous/157/Papers/world_economy.pdf). The data used are from the 2018 update (https://www.rug.nl/ggdc/historicaldevelopment/maddison/releases/maddison-project-database-2018). The purpose of this work is to collect information about economy over the years for all countries.

Visualisation

1. Countries and years

The 169 countries highlighted in blue are considered in the 2018 Maddison World Economy Report (193 countries are member states of the United Nations in 2019). However, some countries had different size or name over the history (i.e. Former Yugoslavia is still used in the report).
